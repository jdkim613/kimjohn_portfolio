Background

Business Requirements

Credena has partnered with Avanade to create a patient-centric experience for their pharmacies. Using the Dynamics 365 platform, the client wants to expand their business and technical infrastructure that can scale as they grow.

Summary

The design team collaborated with CRM consultants to design a custom solution for their current service processes. Additionally, we designed 14 mid-fidelity prototype modules for omni-channel experiences that work in conjunction with the patient mobile app to centralize patient health data.
